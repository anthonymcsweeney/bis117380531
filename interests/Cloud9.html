PaaS (Platform as a Service


What is PaaS

Cloud9 Computing “Platform as a Service” provides your business with a cloud based environment with everything your business requires to support the complete lifecycle of multiple windows based applications that you can make available in the “cloud” without the cost and complexity of buying and managing the underlying hardware, software and provisioning


Benefits of PaaS

• Clear, predictable budgeting - Monthly per user fees with no capital outlay from day 1.

• Fully managed solution - Frees your team to focus on your core mission and service delivery.

• Anywhere, any time remote access - 24/7 access to your business systems.

• Fast performance - Powered by future proof technology built for reliability and scalability.
